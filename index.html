Splash & Catch

Score: 0
Time Left: 60s

How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys or WASD to swim around the pool
  • Press SPACEBAR when near a toy to collect it
  • Collect colorful submersible toys before they sink to the bottom
  • Toys get smaller and harder to see as they sink - act fast!
  • Each toy collected = +1 point
  • Success sound when you collect a toy
  • Fail sound when you miss a toy
  • Special sounds for game start and end
  • Choose between 1 or 2 minute game duration
